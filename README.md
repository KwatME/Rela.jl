## Relationship

### Specific

" (comment)

{ (contain)

\> (become)

\+ (make)

\- (break)

/ (increase amount)

\ (decrease amount)

^ (increase function)

v (decrease function)

### Association

- Positive: {, >, +, /, ^,
- Negative: -, \\, v

## Design

Be real, specific, minimal, and modular.
